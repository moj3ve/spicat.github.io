Support iOS 11+

Enable PIP in native YouTube app.

The only way to activate PIP is to dismiss the app (if PIP just appeared for a few seconds then you need to resume the app and dismiss it one more time to make PIP persistent). This is how YouTube developers want PIP to be implemented at the moment. YouTube PIP is currently in A/B testing and we might see better changes in the future.

You can toggle whether PIP should activate when the app entered the background by tapping on your account avatar at top-right then go to Settings > Picture-in-picture.
